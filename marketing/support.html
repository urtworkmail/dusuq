Common questions

About support tickets.

Do I need to log in to submit a ticket?

No. The ticket system is public — just provide your organization name and Dusuq ERP account username so our team has enough context to help.

What's my "account username"?

The username you log into Dusuq ERP with. If you're not sure, your farm's owner or manager can confirm it.

How do I know my ticket was received?

You'll get a reference number like TKT-A1B2C3D4 immediately after submitting — save it to check the ticket's status anytime.

What's the difference between this and the contact form?

The contact form is for sales and general enquiries. Support tickets are for existing Dusuq ERP accounts with a specific issue.
