Common questions

Before you write in.

Is my farm's data isolated from other farms?

Yes. Every account is fully tenant-isolated — your animals, records and financials are never visible to any other farm on the platform.

Can I migrate from spreadsheets or paper registers?

Yes — bulk Excel import is built into the milk entry module, and our team can help structure a one-time migration for larger herds.

Does it work for both cattle and buffalo?

Yes. Breed masters cover cattle, buffalo and goat, and the platform supports mixed herds on the same farm.

Can multiple people on my team use it at once?

Yes — role-based access lets owners, managers, vets, accountants, technicians and milkers each see only what they need.
