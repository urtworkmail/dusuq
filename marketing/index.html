Dairy management, answered

Common questions about dairy management software.

Straight answers, before you talk to us.

What is dairy management software?

Dairy management software is a digital system that replaces registers and spreadsheets for running a dairy farm — tracking animal records, milk production, health and vaccination, breeding, feed inventory and farm accounts in one connected platform instead of five disconnected ones.

What software is used for dairy farm management?

Farms use purpose-built dairy management systems like Dusuq ERP to track herd data, daily milk yield, health events and finances. The right choice depends on herd size, whether you need offline access, and whether it covers accounts as well as animal records.

Is there dairy farm management software built for Pakistan?

Yes — Dusuq ERP is built in Islamabad specifically for Pakistani dairy operations, supporting both cattle and buffalo herds, multi-shed farms, and the accounting structure local farms actually use, not a generic system adapted after the fact.

Can dairy management software replace my Excel sheets?

Yes. Dusuq ERP's milk module supports bulk Excel import so existing records carry over, and from that point on entry, dispatch, health and breeding events are logged directly in the system — no more end-of-month reconciliation across sheets.

Does dairy management software work for both cattle and buffalo?

A good one should. Dusuq ERP's breed masters cover cattle, buffalo and goat, and support mixed herds on the same farm under one login.

How much does dairy farm management software cost?

Pricing varies by herd size and modules used. Dusuq ERP offers a free trial with no credit card required, so you can evaluate it against your actual daily operations before committing.
