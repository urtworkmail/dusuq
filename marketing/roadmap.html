Roadmap

What's coming in Dusuq ERP v2.0.

The next version of Dusuq ERP moves beyond manual entry — CCTV feed integration and IR visualization, paired with RFID tags on every animal, feed continuous data to highly intelligent systems built to help vets and farm owners understand their animals better.

In development Marketing preview — features described below are on the roadmap, not yet shipped
The problem

Today, the system only knows what someone typed in.

Manual entry is accurate the moment it's logged, but it's a snapshot — not a continuous record. A change in an animal's behavior between two milking sessions, or a subtle pattern across a whole herd, is invisible unless someone happens to notice it in person. v2.0 is about closing that gap.

Pillar 01

CCTV Feed Integration & IR Visualization

Live and infrared camera feeds from the shed, connected directly into Dusuq ERP — turning continuous video into structured, usable data.

  • ✓CCTV feeds from existing or new farm cameras integrated directly into the platform
  • ✓Infrared (IR) visualization for round-the-clock monitoring, including overnight
  • ✓Footage feeds highly intelligent systems trained to prepare detailed, personalized reports for any entity — one animal or a whole herd
  • ✓Built to help vet doctors understand animals better, not to replace their judgment

Pillar 02

RFID Tagging & 24/7 Monitoring

Every animal gets an RFID tag, giving computer vision systems a reliable way to identify and continuously track individuals — not just count shapes in a frame.

  • ✓RFID tags installed on each animal, tying video identity to its existing farm record
  • ✓Computer vision models monitor animals continuously, day and night
  • ✓Behavioral and movement patterns build into a historical record per animal automatically
  • ✓No manual observation required to catch what happened while no one was watching

Pillar 03

Highly Intelligent Systems for Every Stage of the Animal Lifecycle

The point of continuous monitoring isn't the footage — it's what highly intelligent systems can build from it: forecasting, diet planning, and analysis that goes far beyond what a periodic manual check could catch.

  • ✓Detailed, personalized reports for any entity — a single animal or an entire herd
  • ✓Forecasting built from continuous historical data, not just point-in-time entries
  • ✓Diet planning informed by ongoing behavioral and health patterns
  • ✓Support across every other stage of the animal lifecycle — breeding readiness, recovery tracking, and beyond

Common questions

About the v2.0 roadmap.

Is v2.0 available now?

Not yet — this page describes what's on the roadmap. Dusuq ERP today covers animal records, milk, health, breeding, inventory, accounts and VetAssist's data-driven Q&A and reporting.

Will I need new cameras?

Where possible, v2.0 is being designed to integrate with existing farm CCTV infrastructure, with IR-capable cameras recommended for overnight coverage.

Does this replace manual record-keeping?

No — it supplements it. Manual entry (health treatments, breeding events, milk yield) stays the authoritative record; monitoring adds continuous behavioral context on top of it.

How does this connect to VetAssist?

The historical analysis and forecasting these highly intelligent systems produce become part of what VetAssist can answer questions about and include in generated reports.

Using Dusuq ERP today gets you ready for v2.0.

Clean, connected data now means monitoring has something solid to build on later.
