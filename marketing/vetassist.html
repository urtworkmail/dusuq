VetAssist

Ask your farm anything. Get a real answer.

VetAssist is a highly intelligent system built into Dusuq ERP with access to every module — animals, milk, health, breeding, inventory and accounts. Ask it about one animal or the whole herd, and it answers from your farm's actual data, not a generic script.

Report generation

Give it an animal ID. Get a report.

Ask VetAssist to generate a report for any animal or herd, and it pulls everything on record for that entity — health history, breeding timeline, yield trend, feed consumption — into one document.

  • ✓Per-animal reports built entirely from your farm's own recorded data
  • ✓Per-herd or per-shed reports for group-level patterns
  • ✓When the data suggests a need, VetAssist researches diet plans or treatment strategies and includes them as a recommendation
  • ✓Vets can request supporting research on any condition or symptom directly, not just as part of a report

Forecasting & analysis

Historical analysis and forecasting, on request.

VetAssist isn't limited to animal health — ask it to analyze trends or forecast forward, on the herd or the farm's finances.

  • ✓Yield trend analysis for an animal, group, or the whole herd over any period
  • ✓Financial forecasting — projected milk revenue, feed cost trajectory, cash position
  • ✓Historical comparisons — this month vs. last, this season vs. last year
  • ✓Early flags when a pattern in the data looks worth a vet's attention
How it works

Grounded in your data, not a guess.

VetAssist is a highly intelligent system that reads directly from your farm's records before it answers — it doesn't answer from general knowledge alone when your data has the actual answer.

  • ✓Queries your farm's animal, milk, health, breeding, inventory and accounts data first
  • ✓Only reaches beyond your data — for diet science, disease research, treatment strategy — when the situation calls for it
  • ✓Every response is scoped to your tenant; VetAssist never sees another farm's data
  • ✓Available to any role with access to the underlying modules it's drawing from

Common questions

About VetAssist.

Does VetAssist replace my vet?

No. VetAssist is built to help vets and farm owners understand their animals better — surfacing patterns, generating reports, and researching options — not to replace clinical judgment.

Can it see data from other farms?

No. VetAssist only ever queries your farm's own tenant-isolated data, exactly like every other module in Dusuq ERP.

Who on my team can use VetAssist?

Anyone whose role already grants access to the underlying modules — a vet can ask about health and breeding, an accountant can ask about finances, and an owner can ask about anything.

What does report generation actually pull from?

Whatever is on record for that animal or herd in Dusuq ERP — health, breeding, milk and inventory history — plus researched recommendations when the data indicates a need.
